How to get to NTK

By car

GPS: 50.1039000N, 14.3905997E

By public transport

You can reach NTK by trams No 8, 18 („Lotyšská“ stop), No 20, 26 (“Dejvická” stop) or by the underground Prague Metro (line A – green, “Dejvická” stop, use the Šolínova/Vysoké školy exit).

From airport

Take the bus 119 from the Prague Airport to “Nádraží Veleslavín” stop. Here you change to the Prague Metro line A in direction to “Depo Hostivař” and step out at “Dejvická” stop.

Alternative transportation: by Taxi* 20 minutes, approx. fare 25 EUR.

From Main Railway Station:

Subway line C (red line) “Hlavní Nádraží” stop direction Háje, change at “Muzeum” (1 stop) to line A (green line) direction Dejvická.

Alt. Transportation: by Taxi*, 10 minutes, approx. fare 10 EUR.

*Uber, Bolt and Liftago operate in Prague

Practical information:

Friday dinner on October 27, 2023 will take place at the Strahov Monastery Brewery

Just a few steps from the iconic Prague Castle, the Strahov Monastery Brewery is hidden in a historic monastery founded in 1142. Master brewers have been crafting amazing beer here for more than 600 years.

You will enjoy the evening with a nice buffet dinner together with an excursion to the brewery.
