How to get to NTK

By car

GPS: 50.1039000N, 14.3905997E

By public transport

You can reach NTK by trams No 8, 18 („Lotyšská“ stop), No 20, 26 (“Dejvická” stop) or by subway (line A – green, “Dejvická” stop, use the Šolínova/Vysoké školy exit).

From airport

Take the bus 119 from the Prague Airport to “Nádraží Veleslavín” stop. Here you change to subway line A in direction to “Depo Hostivař” and step out at “Dejvická” stop.

Alternative transportation: by Taxi 20 minutes, approx. fare 20 EUR.

From Main Railway Station:

Subway line C (red line) “Hlavní Nádraží” stop direction Háje, change at “Muzeum” (1 stop) to line A (green line) direction Dejvická.

Alt. Transportation: by Taxi, 10 minutes, approx. fare 10 EUR.
